# Fieldio User Guide

Welcome to Fieldio! This guide explains how to use the core features for different roles.

## 🏢 For Admins & Dispatchers

### Dashboard Overview
The main dashboard gives you a snapshot of your business:
*   **Stats**: Today's revenue, active jobs, and available technicians.
*   **Activity Feed**: Real-time log of what's happening (jobs created, technicians arriving).

### Scheduling a Job
1.  Navigate to **Schedule** or **Jobs**.
2.  Click **New Job**.
3.  Select an existing **Customer** or create a new one.
4.  Enter the Job Details (Title, Description).
5.  Set the **Date & Time**.
6.  **Assign a Technician** (optional, you can do this later).
7.  Click **Create Job**.

### Dispatching
Drag and drop unassigned jobs from the "Unassigned Pool" onto a technician's timeline in the Calendar view.

### Settings
*   **Company Profile**: Update your logo and address.
*   **Users**: Invite new technicians or office staff.
*   **Notifications**: Customize the emails/SMS sent to customers.

---

## 🔧 For Technicians

### Mobile Experience
Fieldio is designed to work great on your phone.

### Handling a Job
1.  Open the **Dashboard** to see your assigned jobs for the day.
2.  Tap a job to view details.
3.  **En Route**: When driving to the client, tap "En Route". The client will be notified.
4.  **On Site**: Tap "On Site" when you arrive (stops the travel clock).
5.  **Checklist**: Complete the required checklist items (safety checks, tasks).
6.  **Complete**: When finished, mark the job "Complete".

### Inventory
Check your van's stock levels in the **Inventory** tab.
